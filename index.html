Introductory information

The Oaklet company is the market leader in the development of application software interfaces for data analysis of automated process control systems.

In 2022, in order to reduce the capital costs of maintaining its own information infrastructure, the company implemented a pilot project to switch to the Infrastructure as a service (Iaas) model and selects candidates for further work on this project.
